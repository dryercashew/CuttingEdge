Who We Are

Doug Terrel

Professional Contractor

As an owner / operator for 38 years in the construction industry, Doug Terrel founded Cutting Edge Construction Today, LLC (CEC) in 1992. From its inception, CEC has been a full-phase leader in the construction industry, offering its clientele a distinct advantage.

When you choose Cutting Edge, you get a great team of professionals who have a passion for taking your ideas and bringing them to life!

While many construction firms and remodelers are just now learning of –and beginning to use- ‘green’ materials in their construction, CEC was ‘green’ long before it became fashionable. As Doug Terrel observed, “We were remodeling ‘green’ long before we heard that term. We believe in our future, your future, and in the future generations to come.
